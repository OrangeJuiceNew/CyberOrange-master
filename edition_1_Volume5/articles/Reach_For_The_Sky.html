Who is Sky Browne

Sky Brown is the youngest professional skateboarder in the world. She gained fame after competing in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics on Team Britain under the women's skating event at the age of 13 and ended up placing 3rd. As of this writing, she has roughly 1.3 million Instagram followers, but the crazy part is she started skating by watching YouTube videos when she was just 3 years old. She is also sponsored by Nike, making her the youngest person the company has sponsored. She is also supported by Almost Skateboards and Skateistan. Sky Brown has become a household name in the skating community because at such a young age she is breaking so many records and making a name for herself. Imagine: If she's only 13 and competing in the Olympics and placing 3rd, where will she be 10 years from now?
